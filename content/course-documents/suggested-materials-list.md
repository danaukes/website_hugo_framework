# Suggested Materials List

## Prototyping

* Posterboard[^1] 
* Cardstock  
* Rubber Bands
* Stapler / Staples
* String or Twine 
* Duct Tape
* Rivets 

## Measurement

* Kitchen Scale 
* Fish Scale    
* Tape Measure  
* SmartPhone    
* Multimeter    

## Electronics

* Microcontroller
* RC Servo
* IMU


[^1]: avoid common cardboard(chipboard).  Its layers are too flaky and easy to damage.
